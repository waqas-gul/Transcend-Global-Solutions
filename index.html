Who We Are

Redefining Africa's global engagement

Transcend Global Solutions (TGS) Inc. is redefining how the world engages with Africa. We bring together a team of experienced policy experts, business strategists, technologists, and diaspora leaders with decades of combined experience in international development, trade, diplomacy, and sustainable investment.

Our Vision

To become the premier gateway connecting global capital, innovation, and expertise with Africa's emerging markets, creating transformative partnerships that drive inclusive economic growth and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower African countries, businesses, and diaspora investors by facilitating strategic partnerships, providing expert policy guidance, and deploying innovative climate-smart and AI-driven solutions that unlock Africa's economic potential.

Founder & CEO

Paul K. Kennedy is the Chief Executive Officer of Transcend Global Solutions (TGS), a Washington, D.C.-based international development and trade advisory firm dedicated to strengthening Africa's economic resilience and global competitiveness.
